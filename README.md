Arrakis...Dune....Desert Planet.




Ready yourself to leave the comforting Greens and Blues of Vintage Story's base setting and venture out into the wastes of the Planet Arrakis, also known as Dune. Can you survive where so many others have failed? You will know Thirst like no other, as you have never experienced. You will come to see the light from the Sun, not as a giver of life but as an ever deadly foe. You will feel the quaking of the ground below your feet as you feel the coming and going of the great Worms. You will desperately seek the morning dew and drink it off a crumpled leaf. The blood of your enemies, their water, will sustain you. You will know desperation. You will know suffering. You will know what it means to need. It will break you or forge you stronger.

What's in the mod, you ask?

Pain.

You've heard of animals chewing off a leg to escape a trap? What will you do to escape?

Fear is the Mind-Killer...




This is an Overhaul mod that fundamentally changes Vintage Story in both setting and mechanics. Within VSDune you will find yourself upon a world of no streams, lakes, or oceans but in their place vast open sands with peaks of rock stabbing into the heavens. The Sun and its heat will be your greatest enemy. You will need to seek water from the new morning dew mechanic, or deep within caves, in order to stave off the immense thirst of this world. The Geriatric Spice Melange can be found out in the open sands; It is a precious substance sought by all. The Spice extends life. The Spice expands consciousness. The Spice is vital to space travel. You will see the violet plumes and hear the thunder of distant Spice Blows. This will be the signal that fresh spice has erupted from the ground and can be harvested. You must move fast. The Spice is too important to leave. It will quickly become everything to you: your food, the fibers to make clothing, even plastics to craft the items needed for your survival. Be warned: Where there is Spice, there is Shai-Hulud.

Bless the Maker and his Water....




Shai-Hulud is but one of the many things that will kill you unrelated to the heat of this world. Upon this planet exist a variety of factions, all seeking the same thing: Control of the Spice....for he who controls the Spice, controls the universe. It is the year 10,175, The known Universe is ruled by the Padishah Emperor, Shaddam IV. Under The Emperor's decree, the Noble House Harkonnen has been given a Quasi-Fiefdom and Stewardship of Arrakis, the only planet in the Universe which The Spice Melange can be found. The Harkonnen have ruled Arrakis with an Iron Fist for 60 years. Their patrols scour the desert sands by Ornithopter seeking those who are enemy of the Imperium and who would steal away the Spice they are there to harvest.

Their chief targets, and those who strike as a thorn in many offworlder's sides are the Fremen, natives who have inhabited Arrakis for generations untold. They hide away, their true numbers unknown, out in their Sietches and strike when they have the opportunity. They have made their peace with the Desert World on which they call home. They have mastered surviving the heat through their unique Spice-made technologies oft imitated by the Offworlders...such as Stillsuits, Sand Compactors, and Blood-Water filters. Surviving this world has made them strong and fierce. It is said that their elite could stand in victory over even the Emperor's dread Terror Troops, the Sardaukar.

Caught in between their status as Offworlders and Natives, there are others who can be found roaming the sands of Arrakis...The Smugglers are those who operate under the protection of political agreement and subterfuge to gather the Spice in ways off the official means. They provide ample off-record pay to the Harkonnens and the Guild in order to make their profits outside the watch of the Emperor and the Landsraad. They know to stay out of the way of the Harkonnen, lest they be put down so that the appearance of full loyalty might remain. Others who are not quite offworlders anymore, but not Fremen either. They exist as bandits and scavengers, picking at the bones of wrecks and past conflicts...digging into the sands for long-abandoned Ecological stations that might have scraps of something worthwhile, something they can trade or use, something to help them last just one more day out in this accursed place.

Rarely one may find the agents of the other Great and Minor Houses of the Landsraad, the Subjects and Stewards of the Imperium, upon Arrakis....at the hub of the Universe, wishing to influence its spin.

Plans within Plans...




Your primary goal is to survive this place. As you find your place, you may be accepted within Sietch and Village as if you were Fremen...or perhaps you will become as another Smuggler and make your riches off winds of Cinnamon. Whatever your goals become, first you must live.

Game Guide:




There isn't a Handbook written up for this yet, so I will cover some of the basics:

The Sun and Thirst will kill you very fast. Go by night, in the day stay in shade as much as you can. You start with a stillsuit, so you can drink from it (check the controls in the menu to set them as you wish).
Spice is pretty much required. Listen and look out for spice blows.
Fremen will not attack you (for now). Everyone else will.
Caves are your friend, they have mushrooms and maybe even water.
Spice can make food. Spice can make Plastic. Spice can make a wood substitute and fiber.
Firepits can be made with sticks.
The default progression of tool will eventually be replaced.
Dew appears in the morning on plants but evaporates quickly.
Thopters require a key item to fly. When flying, you can take off with W, Land with G, and then exit by holding shift while landed. When in the air, you can tap CTRL while pressing W to go into the faster glide mode. You cannot gain height and you will eventually fall to the ground in this mode, but it is faster than hovering. Right now you can only steal thopters. Keys can be found on enemies from the Imperium.
The Harkonnen Patrol the skies and will come to attack if they spot you.
Spice blows only in the same area the worms show up in. You have around 7 mins before one will arrive. It will kill you if you are on the sand when it arrives. There will be more warning as the mod is developed.
Spice can be made into clay. Dry and Wet spice have different qualities.
Highly suggest you use Radial Crafting to see what all you can make with spice easily. The food it provides is a key nutrition source.
Spice will fill your Spice Blood Saturation meter. You can use this meter to activate the Eden Instinct Powers.
The Classes are TEMPORARY. The Fremen is unbalanced as a test feature. Don't expect this in full release.
The worm right now is Janky, the animations need work. It will come in time.
There aren't really any goals right now other than to survive.
The mod will change GREATLY over time. Please be aware of that. Saves will probably become invalid.
The Chat Callouts regarding the patrols and the Worm and such are placeholder/Debug stuff that sit in the place of future systems. Same with the HUD, the Open Desert/Worm Danger HUD warning will be adjusted.
The sand fill of the open desert can hide pits where the sand will swallow you.
Lots of small changes to worldgen are coming. Especialy the basin fill level. This will be pretty drastically different in the future.
